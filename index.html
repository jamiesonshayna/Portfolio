Here & Now Project

Developing within a Scrum team to create a cross-platform mobile app for local non-profit the Here And Now Project. The purpose of this app is to empower and connect members of the paralysis community through information, resources, and events. The project is being designed with Flutter and specifically designed for ease of use and accessibility for members who are quadriplegic or limited in motion. Download Here & Now Project for free on your Android phone or tablet, and let us know about any bugs, questions, feature requests, and/or other suggestions.
